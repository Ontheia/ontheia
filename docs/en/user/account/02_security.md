# Password & Security

Secure your account by regularly updating your password.

## Change Password
If you want to change your password, you must provide your current (old) password for confirmation.

### Requirements:
- **Length:** At least 8 characters.
- **Complexity:** We recommend a mix of upper/lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

### After the Change:
- You remain logged in to your current session. 
- If you suspect that your account has been compromised, contact an administrator to terminate all active sessions on the server side.

# Access & Authentication

Ontheia is a multi-tenant system. To secure your personal agents, projects, and histories, a login is required.

## 1. Login
Enter your registered email address and password to access your account.
- **Session Duration:** After a successful login, you remain logged in for 12 hours by default.
- **Security:** Your login data is transmitted encrypted to the host service.

## 2. Signup
If you don't have an account yet, you can create a new profile via the registration link.
- **Required Data:** Email address, password, and optionally your display name.
- **Standard Role:** New accounts automatically receive the `user` role. Admin rights must be manually assigned by an existing administrator.

## 3. Password Security
Should you forget your password, please contact your administrator, as Ontheia currently does not support an automated "forgot password" flow via email (Privacy-by-Design).
